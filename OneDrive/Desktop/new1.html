Q: How do I adopt a pet?

A: Follow the adoption process outlined above. Start by browsing our available pets and submit an adoption form.

Q: What are the adoption fees?

A: Adoption fees vary based on the pet and cover vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other care costs.

Q: Can I volunteer even if I can't adopt?

A: Absolutely! We welcome volunteers to help with various tasks. Please see our volunteer section for more details.
